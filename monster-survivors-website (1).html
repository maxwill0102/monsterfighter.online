About Monster Fighter

Monster Fighter is an action-packed fighting game where you face off against formidable monster opponents. As you defeat enemies, you'll earn experience points to level up and unlock new combat techniques.

The game features multiple fighters to choose from, each with their own unique fighting styles and special moves. Strategically develop your combat skills to create powerful combinations and defeat increasingly challenging monsters.

Inspired by classic arcade fighting games, Monster Fighter offers an exciting experience that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. Do you have what it takes to become the ultimate monster fighting champion?

Tips & Tricks

1. Prioritize Movement Speed

Movement speed upgrades help you avoid enemies and collect experience gems more efficiently.

2. Balance Your Abilities

Don't focus on just one type of ability. Balance offensive, defensive, and utility upgrades for better survival.

3. Collect All Experience Gems

Make sure to collect all experience gems that drop from defeated monsters to level up faster.

4. Keep Moving

Standing still is dangerous. Keep moving in circular patterns to avoid being surrounded by monsters.
